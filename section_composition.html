Composition

Dynamic and innovative, our compositional style is the foundation of all of our visual communications. Composition helps us easily prioritize and organize information in meaningful ways for our customers. It gives our designs a sense of energy and movement. And it allows us to use and combine the elements that comprise our brand palette in a grounded, consistent and integrated way.

Determining the Grid

We want to maintain a consistent optical standard for the border and grid throughout all of our communications. The standard for borders can range from 2% to 5% of the width of the deliverable, depending on the overall size of your communication piece. The grid should be comprised of a series of squares, each of which are 10 to 15% of the width of the deliverable.
