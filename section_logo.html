Logo Standards

Clear Space

To preserve the integrity and visual impact of the IGT logo, always maintain adequate clear space around it. The clear space around the logo is an integral part of its design, and ensures the logo can be seen quickly, uncluttered by other logos, symbols, artwork, or text.

Minimum Size

Logo minimum size with trademark™ symbol: 1"

No trademark™ symbol usage: 3/4" or smaller for on-screen applications

When reproducing our logo, consider its size and legibility. A logo that is too small ceases to serve its communicative function. The minimum size for the IGT logo measures 3/4" or 19.05mm. To determine the minimum size on-screen, first take into account the screen resolution. For example, on a 96 dpi flat panel display, the logo must measure at least 75 pixels wide; on a 400 dpi cellphone screen, the logo must measure at least 300 pixels wide.

Maximum Size

Maximum Size with Trademark: 4"

Too big, too much. When using the IGT logo in large-scale formats, it is important only to include the trademark TM symbol up to 4". Anything larger and the trademark symbol will begin to compete against the mark itself.

Placement

Proper placement and use of clear space protect the integrity of our logo. In all our collateral, the IGT logo is used as the one of our primary branding elements.
