Building Gradients

Picking Colors

Gradients should be built from tonal values within one hue. Working from the provided color palettes above, choose a combination that best suits your intended purpose. Organize tonal values from light to dark to find harmonious pairings.

Gradient Angles

Once you’ve chosen your colors, you can then build your duotone. Duotones commonly function as backdrops in composition, thus making subtle gradations between tonal values within one hue is recommended.

Building Duotones

Consistent application is key. Enhancing linear gradients with movement and fluidity through a 45 to 90 degree directional angle is recommended.
