Motion

With the growing ubiquity of digital devices, moving interfaces and adapting technology, motion design has become an important part of a designers creative options. Understanding the elements that mold the IGT brand expression is key to creating a meaningful and engaging design.

Physics

The digital space allows the brand expression to live and breath, but it also must be in tune with the brand’s personality. With this in mind, the physics behind how the rings appear on screen must be consistently applied as to provide an additional layer of recognition to the viewer.

Variations

Consistent motion affords the brand expression a considerable amount of variety and a designer ample creative opportunity. The simple rotation of the rings creates new and appealing forms that may be used to lend focus and/or intrigue to any composition.

In Practice

Move

Positioning the rings strategically, by their center registration, upon the grid, provides a wide range of creative potential. Thoughtfully, aligning left, center or right allows for uniquely crafted compositions.

Embed

Using the same logic and execution as scaling and filling, you can embed the graphic as a pattern into iconic shapes for in nite variations of this graphic expression. When doing this, be sure to keep your color and treatment consistent so that each variation still connects visually with the IGT brand.

Scale

Here again, simply playing with scale is a powerful way to transform the expression. Zooming into the graphic exponentially yields a fresh new look — where the focal point becomes color and pattern. The rings are still evident, but become a secondary element. Incrementally scaling by percentages of 200, allows the element to be cropped create rich dynamic backgrounds.
